Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Overview

Mahjong Boss ("we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect when you play our free Mahjong Solitaire game at mahjongboss.com and how we use it.

2. Information We Collect

Leaderboard entries: When you save a high score, you may enter a display name (up to 32 characters). This name, along with your score, game layout, and completion time, is stored on our servers and shown on the public leaderboard.

Local storage: Your browser stores game statistics (games played, wins, best scores per layout) and preferences (e.g. highlight playable tiles, sound mute) in localStorage. This data stays on your device and is not sent to our servers.

Technical data: Our server may log standard request data (IP address, user agent, referrer) for security and operational purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Display your high score and name on the leaderboard
  • Improve and maintain the game
  • Prevent abuse and ensure fair play
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use minimal cookies. Our game may use localStorage for preferences and statistics. If we use analytics (e.g. Google Analytics), that service may set its own cookies. You can manage cookies in your browser settings.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics or hosting. These providers have their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

Leaderboard entries are stored indefinitely unless you request removal. Server logs are retained for a limited period as needed for operations.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your leaderboard entries by contacting us. You can clear localStorage at any time via your browser to remove local statistics and preferences.

8. Children

Our game is intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us through the website or the contact details provided on mahjongboss.com.
